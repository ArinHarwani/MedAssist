## 📌 Problem Statement

Keeping track of your medical history, ongoing lab reports, active medications, and past doctor prescriptions can be overwhelming and fragmented. In critical emergencies, precious seconds are often lost scrambling for scattered documents or trying to communicate vital medical conditions to first responders and emergency dispatchers.

## 💡 Our Solution

**MedAssist** solves this by centralizing all your health data securely into a single, intuitive interface. Say goodbye to lost reports and forgotten medications. With MedAssist, everything—from your daily supplements to crucial laboratory documents—is directly at your fingertips, and accessible **exactly when you need it**.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📁 Centralized Health Records
Need to keep all your lab reports and medical info in one place? MedAssist securely archives your medical histories, lab reports, prescriptions, and imaging files securely in the cloud via Supabase Storage. Supports PDF, JPG, and PNG uploads.

### 🤖 AI-Powered Insights
Automatically extract crucial data from your lab reports. MedAssist is natively integrated with **Google Gemini AI** to provide instant clinical summaries, intelligently answering your health questions based on your unique medical profile.

### 💊 Medication Management
Keep track of all your active medications, dosages, and daily supplements in an organized, easy-to-read list, ensuring you never miss a crucial medication update.

### 🚨 One-Tap Emergency SOS Dashboard
Instantly broadcast a high-priority SOS alert containing your precise GPS coordinates, address, and critical medical history straight to connected ambulances and hospital dispatchers. Features a dynamic countdown and bypass lock, reducing dispatch time from minutes to seconds.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router) & React 18
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, and Glassmorphism design principles
- **Database & Storage:** Supabase (PostgreSQL with PostGIS)
- **Map Navigation:** React-Leaflet with Mapbox GL Tiles
- **Artificial Intelligence:** Google Gemini API for medical information extraction
